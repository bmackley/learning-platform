Welcome!

View. Learn. Contribute.

We're here to provide a platform for users to access and create high-quality online resources. Why pull from just one source when you can bring together the knowledge of hundreds, and even thousands, of people to improve your learning? If you're here to use the resources already provided, we're glad to have you. If you want to add your own knowledge to create or improve upon a concept already in our database, we're glad to have you, too. Whether you come here to learn, to contribute, or to do both, we hope you'll always be part of the E3 community.

Once you sign up for an account, you'll be able to access all of the subjects, concepts, and resources available. Each concept comes with the following tools:

theaters

Videos

Learn better by watching? Find the video to help you learn more about that new concept you can't wait to study or the one to finally master a topic you're familiar with

assignment

Quizzes

Did you know that actively recalling what you've learned is one of the best ways to review? Our quizzes will show you what areas you've mastered and what areas might need a little more TLC. Track your scores and see your progress

assessment

Recommenders

Want to know more about your online study habits and how they are affecting your performace? Check out our recommenders which present analytics about your online activity to make recommendations for sections to review, videos to watch, and study habits to work on
